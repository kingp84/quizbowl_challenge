# OSSAA Quiz Bowl Rules

## Gameplay Format
- Matches consist of **two halves**, each with tossup and bonus questions.
- **Tossups**: read to both teams; first correct buzz earns points.
- **Lightning Rounds**: Players/Teams will choose a category and answer clues that are related to that category.

## Scoring
- **Tossup correct**: 10 points
- **Bonus correct**: 10 points per clue (10 per lighning round)

## Timing
- Tossups: Players have **10 seconds** to buzz after a tossup is read and a **4 second** stall count to answer after buzz in.
- Lightning Rounds: Teams have **60 seconds** beginning after the Directions and first clue are read.
