# NAQT Rules

## Gameplay Format
- Rounds consist of 25 tossup questions.
- If tossup is answered correctly, then a player/team as the opportunity to see 3 unrelated bonus questions. They are unrelated to the tossup but related to each other.

## Scoring
- Tossups are worth 10 points, with powers worth 15.
- Negs (incorrect buzzes) deduct 5 points.
- Bonuses follow tossups and are worth 30 points or 10 points each.
