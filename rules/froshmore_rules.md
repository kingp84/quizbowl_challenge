# Froshmore Quiz Bowl Rules

## Gameplay Format
- Matches use **shorter rounds** than varsity competition.
- Tossups and bonuses follow the same pyramidal style.

## Scoring
- **Tossup correct**: 10 points
- **Power correct**: 15 points
- **Bonus correct**: 10 points (one bonus per correct tossup)

## Timing
- Tossup answer window: **5 seconds** after buzzing.
- Bonus answer window: **10 seconds** per part.
- All computations (math or science and tossup or bonuse): **15 seconds**.

## Emphasis
- Designed to encourage **early competitive experience**.
- Focus on **fundamentals**: listening, buzzing, teamwork, and bonus collaboration.
